Census Committee

Dear All, Jai Jinendra On the similar lines that a government conducts censuses, Digamber Jain Saitwal proposes to conduct a census every five years - 2023, 2028, 2033, etc. Moreover, we intend to leverage digital technologies and are calling it 'Saitwal Jain Samaj Online Census 2023'. For the initiative to be successful, we need your full cooperation. Join the Census Now ..Read more..

The initiative aims to accomplish the following objectives:

  1. Obtaining information about the population of cities, towns, and villages
  2. To determine the ratio of males to females
  3. Contact information for all members for easy communication and collaboration
  4. Create a directory of our members with their phone numbers and email addresses
  5. Through census efforts, all members will be able to access committee initiatives, such as:
    • Scholarships, Jobs, Mentoring, and more
    • Enabling matrimonial efforts for young people within our community
    • Build an education-based community
    • Fostering a sense of community
    • Will assist in planning new initiatives that are beneficial to all
    • It will make programs more effective and successful.
    • Spread awareness of relevant government policies.
  6. Divide the entire population between various clusters based on certain common characteristics, such as population, reach, and other democratic factors, for example - 700-1000 inhabitants per cluster.
  7. Local WhatsApp groups will be established
  8. Each member of the family must fill out the form.

If you know Saitwal Digambar friends or family, you can also fill out the form on their behalf. Make sure you fill out the information correctly.
