Aacharya Aaryanandi Scholarship Committee

Jai Jinendra

Aacharya Aaryanandi Scholarship Vision & Mission as follows
Vision -
To empower our community more educated, equitable and prosperous into the society.

Mission:

  1. Every girl, boys complete educational support.
  2. Mentoring to students for career development.
  3. Personality and social esteem development of community student's
  4. Networking within community for job offering within our community industries
  5. To motivate student's to acquire jain philosophy alongwith education

Tirthraksha Shiromani 108 Aacharya Aaryanandi Maharaj's big vision was Saitwal community must be well educated. Our saitwal community were come under lower economical band because of which many students were not pursuing education. Acharya Aaryanandi Maharaj's wish and affection for education of Saitwal community take forward by our Akhil Digambar Saitwal Santha from year of 2011 by starting Aacharya Aaryanandi Scholarship for needy and financially week students on a returnable basis. Till the date we have distributed scholarship of amount Rs. 92,33,000/- to 331 no. students. We got very good response via our scholarship scheme and with help of our sanstha's financial assistance our community's many girls and boys become Engineer, Doctor, CA, administrator, artist etc. With our scholarship scheme many student's are working in various immense organisation and following Jainism alongwith professional life by following vision ofAacharya Aaryanandi Maharaj. Our community students have due respect for our sanstha's timely help via scholarship and by virtue which they have started to refund the scholarship and till the date 68 students refunded the Rs. 18,00,000/- as they got job and settlement.

With scholarship scheme our sanstha helped our saitwal community to acquire quality education and empower financial and social standard. This big work of scholarship is possible because of generous donors of our community who come forward and donated their valuable money for the nobel cause of Aacharya Aaryanandi and our Sanstha's vision. Till the date we have raised corpus of Rs. 1 Cr. under Aacharya Aaryanandi Scholarship and with vision of our beloved President Shri Deelipji Gheware we have targeted to raise the corpus upto Rs. 5 Cr. in next five years to give well education to every Saitwal students.

We also encourage you to donate to this initiative.
