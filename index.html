Welcome

Welcome to Akhil Digamber Jain Saitwal Sanstha, a vibrant and closely-knit community of Digambara Jains. Our community is widely known for our commitment to self-improvement and inner peace, reflected in the meaning of our name – Swahitwal, which translates to working for the benefit of our inner self.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to practice and promote teachings of Jainism and to collaborate to empower our community members and strive for the betterment in all aspects of our lives.

History of Saitwal Jains

Saitwal community is believed to have descended from the Kshatriyas who came to the Deccan to protect the Jain sages and places of pilgrimage from the fiercer sort of people, whose leaders were called Bhairavas. The Setavāla community has been divided into two sub-castes based on the difference of occupation - the tailors and the bodice cloth weavers, shop-keepers, and money-lenders.

Although our community is predominantly in the profession of silver ornament making, weaving, oil extraction, and tailoring, we have members from all walks of life. While the community is predominantly concentrated in Maharashtra, their influence can be seen throughout India, with significant populations in Vidarbha, Marathwada, Khandesh, and small proportions in Western Maharashtra , Madhyapradesh as well.

We take pride in our culture, heritage, and customs, which generations have learned from Gurus and by following teachings of 24 Tirthankaras. Our mission is to preserve and promote our community's values and principles, to uplift and empower our community members, and to work towards the path of enlightenment as guided by our . We strive to achieve this by participating in our religious community events, promoting education, and serving our gurus.

At Saitwal community, we believe that unity and togetherness are essential for the growth and development of any community. We are committed to fostering a sense of belonging and brotherhood among our community members, irrespective of their social status, profession, or background.

Thank you for taking the time to learn about our community.
