Welcome to the Jain Center

Welcome to Saitwal, a vibrant and diverse community within the Digambara Jain community in Maharashtra, India. Our name, Swahitwal, reflects our commitment to working for the betterment of our inner selves, a value that we hold dear in everything we do.

Read More

Mission Statement

At Saitwal, our mission is to work for the betterment of our inner selves and the socioeconomic development of our community. We strive to honor our traditions and values while promoting education and entrepreneurship among our members. We welcome all individuals who share our values to join us in our mission and work together for the benefit of our community.

Become A Volunteer

Whether you are interested in event planning, fundraising, or simply lending a helping hand, we welcome all individuals who are passionate about our mission to become a volunteer.
